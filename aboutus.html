Ugajinn Enterprises

Ugajinn Enterprises was founded in 2018 as an answer to the dire shortage of magitek appliances found in Eorzean homes. Originally partnered with Garlond Ironworks as the Ugajinn Accord, the Accord produced several magitek wonders for the cozy abode. To this day, you may find Accord designed appliances - such as the award winning InfernalTherm Toaster Oven - in the homes of discerning connoisseurs.

Faladis Ugajinn

Chief Engineer

Establishing the Ugajinn Accord in 2013, and then later Ugajinn Enterprises, Faladis is an industrous Lalafell. Spurred on by the shortcomings of the Garlean Empire and his subsequent defection, Faladis has serviced Eorzea and the realms abroad with his knowledge of magitek, aetherial conductance, and automata.

Jorge Maximo, Esq.

Workshop Manager

One of the oldest automata produced by Ugajinn Enterprises, Jorge has proven both a capable engineer and rigorous taskmaster. He is often found defending his opinions and positions with "Do you see the size of my helmet? I thought so, your argument is invalid."

Darwin Anderson
